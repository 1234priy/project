The tourists can undertake an assortment of adventure travel tours to Munnar and enjoy the sightings of a naturally endowed Kerala and its attendant picture postcard charm, and the attraction of its myriad variety of travel places and picnic spots.This hill station destination of Munnar in Kerala carries a lot of promise for adventure activities in the form of hiking and camping,boating and canoeing,bird watching tours, etc. Kayaking and catamaran riding trips are also offered as part of the adventure tours in Munnar.
